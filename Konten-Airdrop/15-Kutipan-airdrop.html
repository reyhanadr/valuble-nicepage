15 Kutipan Airdrop Yang Tidak Pasaran

Seringkali saat sedang mengerjakan airdrop, salah satu tugas yang diberikan adalah meminta kamu untuk melakukan kutip retweet pada postingan tertentu dan mention beberapa teman. Terkadang hal ini membuat kamu kesulitan untuk memikirkan kalimat yang harus digunakan dengan harapan akan menjadi nilai plus bagi tim proyek terhadap partisipasi kamu, karena itu beberapa pengguna selalu menggunakan kalimat yang sama seperti sebelumnya atau mengambil langkah mudah dengan menyalin komentar orang lain. Menurut Minval, bagian ini perlu sedikit diperhatikan walaupun terlihat remeh. 

Dari yang Minval lihat, saat ini para airdrop hunter terlalu banyak menggunakan kutipan retweet yang sama, maka dari itu ayo kita coba lebih banyak variasi kalimat untuk kamu yang sudah Mimin Valu siapin dengan bahasa Inggris!


1. I've seen tons of crypto projects and used them too, but I'm really curious about this one. It seems that they have quite a professional team, let's follow the progress!


2. If there's a better word to describe how excited I am to hear something, then it's a perfect fit for this one project!


3. Previously, I always underestimated a new project, but when I saw this one I was pretty sure that the future of the ecosystem would be good with it.


4. This project has the innovations that today's ecosystem needs. I'm proud to be a part of its development process.


5. A project under development desperately needs a strong and large community to drive the planned vision and mission. I am a part of that!


6. I really believe that this project will be useful for the future of the ecosystem because they have a very strong vision and mission and also they're supported by a professional team.


7. The purpose of this project is very clear as what is in their whitepaper. I think this project will push the crypto industry forward.


8. My initial thoughts upon seeing this project were that the website was professionally built, the utilities offered were useful to some extent. We'll have to see how development goes!


9. It's going to be a great week as a project I admire has launched its airdrop. Wish me luck to be a part of the growth of this project.


10. I admit that this project is so superior in terms of innovation that it is very suitable for long-term investors.


11. I am happy to be involved in the growth of this project through their airdrop event. I have full hopes for its future.


12. I think this project has many long-term features that can be developed continuously, so it is very suitable to be one of the investment options.


13. Even when the market is not doing well, I will still believe in this one project because they have a fair and experienced team.


14. This token will be one of the tokens in my portfolio collection in the long term because they have a clear and attractive vision & mission.


15. Here is one project that is on my recommendation list for you to try. Its utility is clear and it has anti-rug pull projection so it can run in the future to balance the ecosystem.


Kalimat di atas dapat kamu gunakan untuk mengerjakan airdrop dengan tetap menyesuaikan proyeknya supaya cocok ya! Jangan pakai kalimat yang artinya justru tidak sama sekali efisien pada proyek tersebut.
